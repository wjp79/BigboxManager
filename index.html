BigBoxManager for retro PC big box collection management

I made this app to support my hobby. BigBoxManager is a local Windows desktop app built in C# with WPF and SQLite, designed to manage sets, titles, purchases and sales of retro PC big box games, including financial insight, a dashboard and fully local storage without any cloud services.

Overview

Why I started this app

I collect and occasionally sell retro PC big box games. I noticed that general tools did not really fit what I wanted to track. That is why I started building my own local app, focused on my own workflow and interests.

Features

What it currently includes

Set management

Create and manage sets with purchase date, platform, purchase amount, shipping costs, notes and sorting.

Title management

Link titles to sets and store details such as name, platform, language, condition, completeness, image and status.

Financial overview

Track purchases and sales with insight into investment, cost per title, break-even, revenue, profit and remaining amount to recover.

Dashboard

A clear dashboard with key figures for sets, titles, sales, revenue, profit and open investment.

Local storage

All data is stored locally in SQLite. No account, no cloud and no online database required.

Settings and updates

Support for multiple languages, visible app version and a notification when a new version is available.
